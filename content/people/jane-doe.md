# Jane Doe

Jane is a co-founder and CEO of GANG Platform, where she leads the vision for building accessible, performant, and semantic web experiences.

## Background

With over 10 years of experience in web development and accessibility, Jane has been a vocal advocate for semantic HTML and progressive enhancement. She believes that the web should work for everyone, regardless of their abilities or connection speed.

## Current Work

At GANG, Jane focuses on:

- Setting the strategic direction for the platform
- Ensuring all features meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards
- Building a community around accessible web development
- Speaking at conferences about web standards and accessibility

## Philosophy

"The web should be built on documents, not apps. Every page should work without JavaScript, be accessible to screen readers, and load in under 2 seconds."
