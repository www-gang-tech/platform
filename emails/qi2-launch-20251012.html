Qi2 Launch

What's New in Qi2

The key innovation is the Magnetic Power Profile - devices and chargers use magnets to snap into perfect alignment.

Key Improvements

  • 15W guaranteed (vs inconsistent speeds in Qi v1)
  • Perfect alignment every time via magnets
  • Better energy efficiency (less heat waste)
  • Unified ecosystem (no more proprietary fast charging)

Who Benefits?

Everyone, eventually. While iPhones 12+ already support magnetic charging, Android manufacturers are building Qi2 into new flagships.
